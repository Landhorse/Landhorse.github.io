Steps

When starting each track, I followed a general pattern to get to the end result. I've learned these steps through my own experience as well as advice from professionals.

Step 1

Find inspiration from an outside source

Step 2

Develop a chord progression, and add a melody and drumtrack

Step 3

Add additional instruments to differentiate verse from chorus

Step 4

Add effects to tracks to morph the sound, as well as automate the effects to change over time

Step 5

Mix the volumes of all of the tracks in order to make sure that nothing is conflicting with anything else
