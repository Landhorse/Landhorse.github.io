Reflection


As I've mentioned before, my decision to pursue music was suprising, even to me. Most people know me for my love of programming or my times on the track, so choosing to explore this artform seems to be a sign of cognitive dissonance. However, I cannot express how happy I am with my choice. Having this creative outlet has allowed me look at my thoughts in a new light; my ideas staying in plaintext in my head are not as vibrant as they are on sheet music. I find myself also attatching myself to my pieces. While it may seem crazy to most, I write my songs by personifying my instruments: the piano comes in to argue with the bass, the strings are reflecting on their regrets, and the drums are frustrated with their circumstances.
