Description of Work

For my badge this year, I decided to learn about music production. With my
exploration of lyricism last year, it only felt natural to continue in the world of
hip-hop and develop beats. Over the course of the year, I honed much of my
craft, learning how to use new tools and understand music creation on a
fundamental level. I’ve used these new skills to create a small corpus of tracks,
each one illustrating a different growth I’ve had as a creator. In the future, I hope
to put lyrics to each song and perhaps release a project. However, I’m very
happy with how the tracks are in their current state.
