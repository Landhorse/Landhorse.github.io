Description of Work

For my badge this year, I decided to learn about music production. With
my exploration of lyricism last year, it only felt natural to continue in the
world of hip-hop and develop beats. Over the course of the year, I honed
much of my craft, learning how to use new tools and understand music
creation on a fundamental level. I have used these new skills to create a
small corpus of tracks, each one illustrating a different growth I have had
as a creator. In the future, I hope to put lyrics to each song and perhaps
release a project. However, I am very happy with how the tracks are in
their current state.
